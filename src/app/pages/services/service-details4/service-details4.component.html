Customer Service

Enrol
Everyone sells one thing or the other. It could be a product or a service. In doing this, the importance of customer relationship cannot be underestimated. This course helps one to understand how to relate with customers in a friendly but professional way. It educates on how to get or lure customers, clients, manage difficult customers/clients and how to turn "expired" customers/clients into active ones and retain them. It also teaches the right terms to use in relating with customers/clients and how to keep them happy and satisfied.
